Frequently asked Questions (FAQ)

Why should an applicant register with Karyya.com ?

Karyya.com is an unique Job search portal , helps applicant to find the right job for his / her requirement.

How can applicant post his / her Resume ?

Applicant need to sign up first

Post sign in, the next step is of filling up applicant personal and professional details. Next Step, attach the CV / Resume / Biodata and submit to upload his / her resume.

How can applicant update/edit my Profile ?

a. Applicant can update your profile only after login to Karyya.com.

b. Applicant can make changes to his/her Profile by editing the relevant sections.

c. It is recommended that Job seeker update his / her Profile regularly.

d. Recruiters often contact only those candidates who have recently updated their profiles.

Does it cost to post Resume?

NO. Absolutely free of cost for Applicant.

What are Security advises for users?

The user of this web site is bound by Terms and conditions that the Employers must agree and adhere to.One of the primary objectives of these Terms and Conditions is to discourage and prevent misuse and fraud.

False job postings or non-existing job offers are illegal.

Collection of personal information and/or money from unsuspecting job seekers are criminal offence.Karyya.com does not encourage followings:

a. Karyya.com do not ask Jobseeker personal, non-work related information such as Credit Card/ Debit card numbers or Bank information over phone or email.

b. Karyya.com do not provide valid contact information to ask for monetary transactions, money-transfers, or payment for any employment/recruitment related purpose promise emigration and ask for money etc.

Advices to Job seeker do not reveal their personal informations with fraudulent callers / Email requests. Beware for fraudster Phishing attempt and Spoofing attempt.If you have received (or receive) any such suspicious email communication from a possible Karyya.com, do not hesitate to report it to us at contactus@Karyya.com. You can also block the employer from contacting you in future.

How Employer can post a job on Karyya.Com ?

Posting jobs on Karyya.Com is simple and easy :

a. Employer needs to create account at Karyya.com with the username and password.

b. Go to Job Postings page and Post a Job

c. Fill the Job Posting form, preview, Edit if required and resubmit.

d. Caution: After payment has been validated / received by Karyya.com team, Employer can start posting jobs immediately.

e. If Employer feels any challenges, please contact us at contactus@Karyya.com

How long will Employer posted jobs remain advertised on the Karyya.com web site?

All posted jobs will be remaining active for 30 days/ till validity of subscription.

What happens to employer job after 30 days ?

The jobs are marked active for 30days / active period. Beyond 30days / active period, that job marked as Inactive Jobs. These jobs are still available in system database for next 6 months; Employers can readvertise these jobs when required with fresh payment and confirmation.

How Employer can remove the job from web site?

Employer can withdraw at any point of time as per their requirement.

a. Go to the manage jobs section

b. Click on the Remove / Delete icon against the job which employer wants to withdraw.

Is Career Advice Available in web portal for user information?

Yes. Career Avdvice is available in web portal for user reference.

Beware of Fraudsters !

a. www.karyya.com does not promise a job or an interview in exchange of money from Job seekers.

b. Caution ! Fraudsters may ask you to pay in the pretext of registration fee, Refundable Fee.

Ways to protect your self from Fraudsters !

a. Refuse make any payment for any Job offer.

b. Do no disclose your bank details / OTP/ any trasanctions Etc.

c. Never respond without verifying from www.karyya.com

Jobseeker Registration Process

1. Click https://www.karyya.com/Jobsekker/register

2. Enter your E-Mail ID and Phone

3. Generate OTP and Enter OTP for security verification

4. Select Experience

5. Attach Your CV / Resume

6. Read Captcha carefully and Fill Captch in section

7. Select Terms & Conditions, Subscribe News Letter

8. Submit for Registration

Jobseeker Profile Completion Process

Jobseeker Profile Completion Process is basically FIVE Steps.

1. Attach your latest Photo

2. Fill and complete My Personal Profile section

3. Fill and complete My Quali.,Exp. & CV section

4. Fill and complete My Job Preferences section

5. Fill and Complete My Projects section

Additional Features:

1. View Preview

2. View Profile Score to check the sections filled and completed & remaining section to be filled and complete.

Employers Registration Process

Employers Registration Process:

1. Click https://www.karyya.com/recruiter/register

2. Fill company HR ( Representative ) Name, E-Mail, Phone, Company Name etc..

3. Fill HR Contact Information

4. Please Enter Captcha

5. Accept Terms and Conditions, Subscribe News Letter

6. Submit for Registration Completion.

Employers Profile Completion Process

1. Upload Company Logo ( Fit the Size)

2. Complete Company Info, Fill Company website address, Select Industry type, Select Skills Type

3. Paste brief Company Profile to be viewed by Jobseekers.

4. Fill and complete Contact Person Information

5. Fill and Complete Address / Location with PIN code

6. Fill and Complete Company Social links

7. Save the Profile.
